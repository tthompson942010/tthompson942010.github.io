About Trevor Thompson

I have been in web development for around ~1 year, starting my education at the UCF Coding Bootcamp. While there, I gained experience with a number of technologies, such as nodeJS, React, Angular and PHP.

I'm also well versed in many popular libraries, such as Bootstrap, Foundation, and jQuery.

Regarding database development, I have worked with both mongoDB and mySQL to create and store data, and have experience with a number of APIs under my belt.

I work well by myself, but find working with others more rewarding, as sharing knowledge is very important to me, both in terms of helping others learn and learning myself. I appreciate a challenge, and want to work to build new and unexpected things.
